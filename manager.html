About Us

My Dear Staffs, Parents & Students,


I am delighted to greet the birth of this Institution in the hope that an ever strong hand of loyality and co-operation will write our staff , our parents and our students through the years ahead . NBSS is being established with its nobel objective of providing ann education that aims at all-round development of the children commited to its care .

Education can bring light . it can empower the individual to confront the inner darkness and with courage dispel it . In this way one discoverss one’s hidden potential and manifest it through creative celebration .

The 14th century German mystic Meister Eckert has said. "Become aware of what is in you , pay attention to your inner life and then announce it , pronounce it, produce it and give birth to it." Education can make this possible . If education can make every person aware that one is enough as one is , and can enable him to experience the bliss of it , if education can make facilities available for the full growth of what one has facilities for growth , not for ambition; facilities for love , not not competition ; facilities for self-awakening and consciousness , not for conflict with other-then such education will be able to bring about a fundamental resolution in the world .
NBSS is conceived to make education more enjoyable , life sustaining , creative and relevant.

We cherish the fond hope that your co-operation and appreciation in our effprt to provide total education to our children , will help them bring about not just a physical or psychological but a spiritual transformation as well .


Ajay Shukla
Manager
